Discover Kendama

What is Kendama?

Kendama is a traditional Japanese skill toy/game. Recently it has been introduced to more people around the world. The community behind the game is getting bigger and bigger by the years!

The principle of these toys are the same: catching one object with another, where both are joined by a string. However the modern kendama style takes influences from a diverse range of skills including yo-yo, diabolo, juggling, and poi.
